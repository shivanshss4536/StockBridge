The Problem

Why inventory mismanagement keeps costing you

Without real-time visibility, every order is a gamble. The status quo fails small shops in three predictable ways.

📦

Over-Ordering Ties Up Capital

Without knowing actual supplier stock, shops over-order "just in case." Cash sits on shelves instead of growing your business.

⚠️

Stockouts Kill Sales

Suppliers run out and don't notify you. Customers walk in, products are missing, and sales are permanently lost — not just delayed.

📞

Endless Back-and-Forth

Calling suppliers to check availability, waiting for callbacks, chasing up orders — hours of admin that should be seconds.

Features

Everything a shop owner needs

📡

Real-Time Stock Feeds

Live inventory levels from all your suppliers in one unified dashboard. Updated the moment stock changes.

🔔

Smart Low-Stock Alerts

Set custom thresholds per product. Get notified via app, SMS, or WhatsApp before you run out.

🛒

One-Click Reordering

Auto-populated order forms based on your reorder rules. Place orders across multiple suppliers simultaneously.

📊

Demand Forecasting

Historical sales patterns combined with supplier lead times predict what to order and when — before you run low.

🔗

Universal Supplier Integration — No Code Required

StockBridge connects to 200+ supplier systems via pre-built integrations. No technical expertise needed. If your supplier uses WhatsApp, email, or a portal, we connect to that too via our lightweight supplier app.
