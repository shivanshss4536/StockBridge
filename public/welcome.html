01

Quick Setup

The first step is connecting your supply network. Without suppliers, StockBridge cannot automate your orders.

1. Add a Supplier

Go to the Suppliers tab and click "+ Add Network". Enter your supplier's WhatsApp number to enable one-click reordering.

2. List Your Products

In the Inventory tab, add your items. Make sure to set a "Low Stock Warning Limit" so the AI knows when to alert you.

Zero-Click WhatsApp Orders
